Our Services

DATA DRIVEN CULTURE
Regardless of the size of their data, all organizations need a more data driven culture. We will help your organization achieve that data driven state of mind that will drive its strategy to success with our Data Driven Value Chain process.

BIG DATA ANALYTICS
Custom Big Data Analytics adapted to the needs of your organization.

  1. Hadoop ecosystem for your Big Data needs with, Spark, MapReduce, Hive, Pig, Kafka, HBase, Sqoop, Mahout.
  2. Big Data stream processing using YARN, Spark Streaming, Storm, Cassandra, Redis.
  3. Big Data in the cloud. Leverage the power of the Elastic Cloud for your Big Data needs with tools such as Amazon Kinesis, Amazon Simple Storage Service (Amazon S3), Amazon Elastic Map Reduce (Amazon EMR), or Amazon Redshift.

LARGE SCALE COMPUTER VISION
We combine our expertise in Big Data Analysis with our experience in Computer Vision. We will help your organization to process large amounts of images coming from multiple sources such as Medical Imaging, Satellite Images, Security Images, etc. Some examples of what we can do with your images include:

  1. Object Detection.
  2. Image Segmentation.
  3. Image classification.
  4. Feature detection.
  5. Image combination.
