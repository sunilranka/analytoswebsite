Strategy

Big Data is all about finding a needle of value in a haystack of unstructured information. Companies are now investing in solutions that interpret consumer behavior, detect fraud, and even predict the future. Big Data is everywhere. Customer Data, Social Networking Data, External Watch Sites, Industry Lists. IDC projects that by 2020 the digital universe will encompass a staggering 35 zettabytes of data. The disparate variety of data - including next-generation operational technology (OT), video streams, images, audio and social networks, increased velocity in which this data is captured and analyzed, and the growing volumes requires an approach that is different from the traditional approaches followed.

Nous has partnered with Hortonworks, a leading commercial vendor promoting the innovation, development and support of Apache Hadoop. Analytos extensive IT industry experience and Big Data expertise, coupled with Hortonworks’ proven experience in implementing and supporting Apache Hadoop in production environments, will offer best-in-class solutions to all enterprises faced with Big Data challenges.

Four Dimensions of Big Data: Big data solutions that have the capability to manage variability, velocity, variety and volumes of data are seen as the solution that can help in managing new age information.

The ability to cost effectively store and process all these sources of data and develop insights is key, and this can be solved using big data platforms.

Analytos nRADAR™ Framework: nRADAR™ (near Real Time Data Archival And Retrieval”) is proprietary framework of Nous that enables users to archive & retrieve data in a reduced time frame as against the time consuming legacy systems. This unique framework was founded by Nous to cater to the vast requirements of the exponentially increasing volumes of Data.
