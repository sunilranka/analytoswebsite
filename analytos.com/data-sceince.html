Data  Science

PLAN

Analytos team begins with a hypothesis and an evaluation of the data and workloads that support your business objectives. Sprint plans and technology needs are identified.

PROVE

Analytos team develops prototypes to validate the hypothesis. If results are promising, the team focuses on designing for business data insights.

PILOT

Analytos team helps with building Statistical mathematical models, data infrastructure, and data feeds. Team works with customers to educate their team about the technology and decision-making opportunities from the data.

PRODUCTION

Customer's systems are scaled to support production workloads while operational management for Customer's solution is transferred to customer internal team.
